1. Disposable Period

Daily disposable lenses offer the best comfort because every pair is a new pair.
Reusables are economical and requires hygienic habits.
---

2. Prescription Type

Short or far sighted prescriptions tend to look shorter. ( eg: -2.25 )
Astigmatism prescriptions are longer with 3 numbers. ( eg: -2.25/-1.25 x 90 )
Multifocals prescriptions will have an ADD. ( eg: -2.25 ADD +1.50 )
---

3. Wearing Hours Per Day

Silicone Hydrogels (SiHy) are premium materials, allowing more oxygen to get to the eyes.
Hydrogels (Hy) have been around for decades and is the normal standard.
Eyes are less red and tired if they have enough oxygen.
---

4. Surface Technology

Premium designs have enhanced comfort from advanced coating, material and solution buffer.
Standard designs remain effective but offers less long-term comfort.
